    How do I book cheap flight tickets?

    Here's how you can book cheap flight tickets

    • Book your flights from ixigo in advance to get the cheapest deals.
    • Be flexible, and consider flying during off-peak hours to get cheaper flight tickets.
    • ixigo's fare alerts feature sends you notifications when the air ticket price on your route gets cheap.
    • If you have time, try stopover flights as flights with multiple stops are often cheaper.
    • Avoid traveling on weekends, as air tickets are usually higher during this time.
    • Try budget airlines like Indigo, Air India to book cheap flight tikets.
    What are the benefits of flight booking with ixigo?

    The benefits of booking with ixigo include cheap fares, easy ticket booking, real-time flight status tracking, exclusive flight ticket offers, flexible date options, auto web-checkin and quick refunds.

    How do you make flexible online flight ticket booking with changeable dates?

    Select your preferred flight and opt for 'Assured Flex' on the 'Review Flight' page to make flexible online flight ticket bookings with changeable dates. This will allow you to modify your travel dates

    Can I modify or cancel my booking?

    Yes, you can modify or cancel your flight with Assured and Assured Flex fares. Assured offers free cancellations on new bookings, while Assured Flex provides free cancellations or one-time free rescheduling, including date, airline, and route changes.

    Can I upgrade my seat after booking?

    Yes, you can upgrade your seat by paying an additional fee. You can also choose a seat during web check-in, which usually begins 48 hours before the flight's departure.

    Are there any special fares for children, infants, or senior citizens?

    ixigo offers up to a 10% discount on base fares for senior citizens aged 60 and older. It also offers up to a 10% discount on base fares and extra baggage allowance for students aged 12 and above..

    Can I book additional baggage through ixigo?

    Yes, you can book additional baggage through ixigo by selecting the option during flight booking or managing your booking later, depending on the airline's policy. This is applicable on ixigo for some airlines like IndiGo, Akasa Air, etc.

    How to check flight status on ixigo?

    To check flight status, open the flight status page, enter your flight details, and view real-time updates on your flight.

    How can I get domestic flight offers on ixigo?

    To get domestic flight offers on ixigo:

    • Visit the ixigo website and enter your travel dates and destination.
    • Click on the "offers" tab to view available discounts and promotions on domestic flights.
    • Choose your preferred offer and make the payment at the time of booking using various payment options, including credit and debit cards, net banking, and wallets.

    ixigo offers various cashback offers on domestic flight bookings from time to time.
